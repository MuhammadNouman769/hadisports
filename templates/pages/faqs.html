General Questions

Q What is Hadi Sports?

Hadi Sports is a leading sports retailer in Pakistan, offering a wide range of high-quality sports equipment, footwear, apparel, and accessories for various sports including badminton, tennis, cricket, football, and more.

We are committed to providing the best sports products and services to our customers.

Q Where is Hadi Sports located?

Our main store is located at:

  • Address: 297-C, P.I.A. Main Boulevard Road, Lahore, Pakistan
  • Phone: +92 300 1234567
  • Email: info@hadisports.com
Q What are your business hours?

Our business hours are:

  • Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Orders & Payments

Q How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy:

  • Browse our products and add items to your cart
  • Go to the cart and proceed to checkout
  • Fill in your shipping and payment details
  • Confirm your order

You will receive an order confirmation email after placing your order.

Q What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Debit Cards
  • Bank Transfers
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
Q Is my payment information secure?

Yes, your payment information is completely secure. We use:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI-compliant payment gateways
  • Secure data storage practices

We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

Shipping & Delivery

Q How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on your location:

  • Lahore: 1-2 business days
  • Other Major Cities: 2-4 business days
  • Remote Areas: 4-7 business days
Q Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping on orders above Rs. 2,500. For orders below this amount, a standard shipping fee of Rs. 150 applies.

Q Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our courier partner's website.

Returns & Refunds

Q What is your return policy?

Our return policy includes:

  • Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • Return shipping costs are borne by the customer
  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
Q How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return:

  • Contact our customer support team
  • Provide your order number and reason for return
  • Follow the return instructions provided
  • Ship the item back to us
Q What items are non-returnable?

The following items are non-returnable:

  • Final sale items
  • Personalized or customized products
  • Items damaged due to improper use
  • Items that have been used or washed

We offer competitive pricing for schools, clubs, and organizations.

Account & Support

Q How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple:

  • Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button
  • Fill in your name, email, and password
  • Agree to our terms and conditions
  • Click "Create Account"
Q I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you forgot your password:

  • Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
  • Enter your registered email address
  • You will receive a password reset link via email
  • Follow the link to create a new password
